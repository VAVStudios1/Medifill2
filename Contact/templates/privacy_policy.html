MediFill Privacy Policy

Introduction

At MediFill, we are committed to protecting the privacy and security of our users. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of personal information that we collect, how we use and protect that information, and the choices you have about how we use that information.

Information Collection and Use

We collect personal information from you when you use our website, including:

  • Your name and contact information
  • Your medical history and health information
  • Your location and appointment scheduling information
  • Your payment information for medicine purchases
  • Your role-based access information (e.g. doctor, shop keeper)

We use this information to provide you with the services and features of our website, to improve our website, and to contact you with important information.

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your personal information with trusted third parties who assist us in operating our website or providing services to you. These third parties are bound by confidentiality agreements and are not permitted to use your personal information for any other purpose.

We may also disclose your personal information if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to comply with legal process, protect and defend our rights or property, or act in emergency situations to protect the personal safety of our users or the public.

Data Security

We take appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access to or unauthorized alteration, disclosure, or destruction of data. These include internal reviews of our data collection, storage, and processing practices and security measures, as well as physical security measures to guard against unauthorized access to systems where we store personal data.

Data Integrity

We review our data collection, storage, and processing practices to ensure that we only collect, store, and process the personal information needed to provide or improve our services. We take reasonable steps to ensure that the personal information we process is accurate, complete, and current, but we depend on our users to update or correct their personal information whenever necessary.

Access to Personal Information

You have the right to access the personal information that we have about you. You may request a copy of your personal information by contacting us at privacy@medifill.com. We will respond to your request within a reasonable timeframe.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post any changes on this page and, if the changes are significant, we will provide a more prominent notice (such as a banner). We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy regularly for any changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@medifill.com.
